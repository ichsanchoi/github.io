Project Details

Actors: Social Worker, Head Division, Psychologist
The PMKS web was developed with the aim of making it easier for the Malang city social services to provide services and camp assessment operations for people with social welfare problems (PMKS).

These are features of the project:
  • Registration
  • Dashboard
  • Addition of PMKS data
  • psychological guidance for PMKS
  • PMKS reporting
  • Document printing
  • Social Workers scheduling
